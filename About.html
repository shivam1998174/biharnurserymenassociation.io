About Us

About Bihar Nurserymen Association

Together, we work towards combating climate change and conserving natural resources. Join us in our mission for a greener future.

Who We Are

In the current scenario of climate change and depleting groundwater, weather patterns are disrupting nature's balance. These disruptions pose significant risks to life on Earth.

We, the Nurserymen & Progressive Farmers, have taken this mission into our own hands. As Green Warriors, we aim to lead large-scale plantation drives and groundwater conservation programs with various organizations.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision

  • To bring all people working in Nursery & its Allied sector under one platform to foster business development.
  • To transform the Nursery Business in Bihar from unorganized to an organized industry.

Mission

  • To convert Bihar from a plant purchaser to a plant producer & supplier of horticultural & ornamental plants.
  • To uplift nurserymen businesses and create employment through skill development.
  • To increase awareness of plantation among farmers & students to make them Green Warriors.

The Bihar Nurserymen Association is committed to promoting sustainable horticulture practices, environmental conservation, and large-scale plantations across the state.
