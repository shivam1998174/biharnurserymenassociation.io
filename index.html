About Bihar Nurserymen Association

In the current scenario of Climate Change & depleting Ground Water, the weather patterns are disrupting the usual balance of nature. This pose many risks to human beings and all other forms of life on earth. Changes in temperature causes changes in rainfall & this leads to several problems like, drought in some areas & floods in others. The only solution to this problem is large scale plantation & conservation of ground water & controlled usages of natural resources. In this mission of saving our mother nature, we all need to contribute & this can happen only when we can connect it with the entire population of the state & the country. We will work on large scale plantation drive & ground water conservation program in association with the Government, Private Institutions, NGOs, Corporates, Schools & Colleges.

Our Key Services

Business Development

We aim to bring all stakeholders in the nursery & allied sectors under one platform for mutual business development and growth.

Organizing the Industry

We strive to transform the nursery business in Bihar from an unorganized sector to a highly organized industry, promoting growth and sustainability.

Green Warrior Movement

We are working to convert Bihar from a plant purchaser to a plant grower, promoting large-scale plantation to make every citizen a green warrior.

Skill Development

We provide skill development programs to uplift the businesses of nurserymen and create employment opportunities through various gardening services.

Water Conservation

We focus on large-scale groundwater conservation programs to ensure sustainable water usage and protect our natural resources.

Awareness Programs

We work on spreading awareness among farmers and students to make them understand the importance of plantation and environmental protection.
