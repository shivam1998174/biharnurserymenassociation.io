About Bihar Nurserymen Association

In the current scenario of Climate Change & depleting Ground Water, the weather patterns are disrupting the usual balance of nature. This pose many risks to human beings and all other forms of life on earth. Changes in temperature causes changes in rainfall & this leads to several problems like, drought in some areas & floods in others. The only solution to this problem is large scale plantation & conservation of ground water & controlled usages of natural resources. In this mission of saving our mother nature, we all need to contribute & this can happen only when we can connect it with the entire population of the state & the country. Now that’s where we Nurserymen & Progreesive Farmers decided to take this task in our hand & we as GREEN WARRIOR will lead from the front in this mission. We will work on large scale plantation drive & ground water conservation program in association with the Government, Private Institutions, NGOs, Corporates, Schools & Colleges.

Vision

• To bring all people working in Nursery & its Allied sector under one platform which will help in mutual business development

• To transform from Unorganized Nursery Business to Organized Industry in Bihar

Mission

• To convert Bihar from Plants Purchaser to Plants Grower/Producer & largest supplier in its native & traditional horticultural & Ornamental Plants across India

• To uplift businesses of Nurserymen & create employment in it through Skill Development in various gardening services

• To increase awareness & importance of plantation among Farmers & students to make them the green warrior
